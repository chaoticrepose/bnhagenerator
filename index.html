My Hero Academia Character Randomizer

Hey Lad, Thanks for checking this out for me!

I got bored and didn't know what to draw so i drew up this nifty lil' program to give me ideas.

There should be 58 characters, including hero costumes, heroes, UA Staff, and villains, making for a total of 3,364 different pair combos possible! But, if you notice a character is missing, just email me and I can get right to it! It's not too hard to add things to the generator, so feel free!

If a blank space shows up, give it a minute to load. If it persists, or the word "Undefined" shows up, click the button again to refresh it. I'm still working through a few kinks here and there, but it's functional and otherwise, runs smoothly. If you notice any other bugs, just text me or lemme know. Thanks, lad!
